Air Filter

V65 Sabre:

In lieu of :17210-MB3-000 ELEMENT, AIR CLEANER $9.38 at http://www.servicehonda.com/

The KN filter for a 91-94 Saturn (KN 33-2055) fits the air box on the V65 Sabre. K&N 33-2034 needs just a little trimming around the gasket.

Purolator A24648 also fits.

But Mr. Dodge says "The V65 Sabre works best with the stock foam filter.

It sells for approx.$12.00 from Honda. All other non-foam filters will choke the intake flow."
