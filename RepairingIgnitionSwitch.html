Repairing Ignition Switch

The switch has two parts: one is the key lock, the other is the electrical switch.

They can be separated:

  • Pull out the whole ignition switch
  • Turn the key halfway between off and on then simultaneously press in all three tangs at the bottom and pull the electric part of the switch from the key lock
    • Be careful not to loose the springs and small parts
  • Check for wear on the contacts. If not too bad, clean, repair and reassemble
    • If the contacts have severe divots, replacement is recommended
